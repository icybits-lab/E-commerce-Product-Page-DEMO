How long does the battery last?

The TechGear Pro Wireless Headphones offer up to 40 hours of playback on a single charge with noise cancellation enabled. With noise cancellation turned off, you can get up to 50 hours of battery life.

Are these headphones compatible with all devices?

Yes, they feature Bluetooth 5.3 and are compatible with all Bluetooth-enabled devices including smartphones, tablets, computers, and smart TVs. They also include a 3.5mm audio cable for wired connection.

Is the carrying case included?

The standard edition includes a basic fabric pouch. The premium carrying case is available as an optional accessory. The Pro and Ultimate editions include the premium case.

What's the warranty period?

All TechGear Pro headphones come with a 2-year manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. You can also purchase extended warranty coverage as an accessory.

Can I use these for gaming?

Absolutely! The low-latency Bluetooth connection makes them excellent for gaming. The built-in microphone with noise reduction ensures clear communication with your teammates.
